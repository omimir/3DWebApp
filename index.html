V

Vitrine

Interactive 3D, in your browser.

Three models built in Blender and run in the browser with Three.js: a drinks can, a water bottle and a desk globe. Orbit them, change the lighting, swap textures and take them apart.

Open the app

What is inside

  • Three models loaded from glTF, with an orbit camera and both perspective and orthographic views.
  • Controls for wireframe, lighting, post-processing and the globe's surface texture.
  • An animation for each model: the can pops open, the bottle's cap unscrews, the globe spins on its stand.
  • A custom GLSL atmosphere shader and an optional post-processing pass.
  • Import your own glTF files straight into the gallery.
